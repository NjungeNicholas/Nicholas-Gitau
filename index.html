Background

I am a student at Machakos University pursuing a degree in Information Technology. Being an undergraduate, I have been able to gain a lot of insights in computer programming, data communication and networks, IT fundamentals, and many more.

I desire to venture into web development and software development in the near future. I am also desiring to learn more about data science and machine learning.

Tech Journey

I started my tech journey in 2024 when I joined Machakos University. I was introduced to several of the tech communities here on campus such as MLSA, GDG On Campus and the ICP Community. Here I have been able to acquire knowledge on Microsoft technologies, Google Cloud, and building web3 applications on the ICP blockchain.

Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors (MLSA)

The Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors (MLSA) program is a global group of on-campus student leaders who are passionate about technology and eager to help fellow students. As an MLSA, I have had the opportunity to engage with a vibrant community of like-minded individuals, participate in exclusive events, and gain access to valuable resources and training. This program has significantly enhanced my technical skills in Power BI, Azure, Github and many more.

Google Developers Group On Campus (GDG On Campus)

The Google Developers Group On Campus (GDG On Campus) is a community of students who are passionate about technology and eager to learn and share their knowledge with others. As a member of GDG On Campus, I have had the opportunity to participate in a wide range of events and workshops, where I have learned new skills on Google Cloud, Kotlin and android development, met like-minded individuals, and gained valuable experience. This community has provided me with a platform to collaborate with others, share my knowledge, and contribute to the tech ecosystem.

Internet Computer Protocol (ICP) Community

The Internet Computer Protocol (ICP) Community is a group of developers who are passionate about building web3 applications on the ICP blockchain. As a member of the ICP Community, I have had the opportunity to learn about blockchain technology, smart contracts, and decentralized applications (dApps). This community has provided me with a platform to collaborate with others, share my knowledge, and contribute to the decentralized web.
